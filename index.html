Who Are We? - Our Story

The first time the five of us met was at the UC Berkeley Business Academy of Youth. Our business idea was stemmed from the same issue we all struggled with: finding internships to enhance our learning. We all tried using job sites to find interships, but there were no services useful for high school students seeking internships. Thus, we decided to create an online service that would create a professional community exclusively for high school students to find internships and intellectual jobs.

Since all five of us came up with the idea and worked together on each aspect of the business plan, we decided that we will all be the CEO, CFO, CMO, COO, and CTO. In the past, there has been conflicts with big companies (such as Facebook) on ownership, so we decided that we each will own an equal part of it. We know that our company will have a large target because we know of friends and classmates who are all in search for high school internships. Our goal is to expand learning beyond the classroom.

For high school students, by high school students.

Our Research & Thank You's


In order to advance our research about the high school internship process & market, we decided to meet with one of UC Berkeley's well known experts on this subject, through Tara's reccommendation. This expert is Devon: the director of the BOOST Program at UC Berkeley, who helps to connect students to internships & prepares first generation students to apply for college. We decided to meet up with Devon to discuss about our business potential & to see if he could help us with developing our service. After making three appointments with Devon, we developed a final idea & plan for our business. We would like to thank Devon for all his help & the University of California Berkeley for allowing us to initiate this company through Bbay, as this service would not become a reality without his help & this program's.
